A Guide for Fathers Who
Want to Show Up With Intention

Guardians of Grit is a book for dads who didn’t get a clear model of what healthy fatherhood looks like—but who are determined to raise their sons with strength, presence, and compassion.

Through stories, questions, and practical guidance, this book helps you build the kind of relationship your son can stand on for the rest of his life.
