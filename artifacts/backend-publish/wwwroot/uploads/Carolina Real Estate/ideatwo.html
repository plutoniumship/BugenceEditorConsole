About Carolina Gandrud

A global perspective with Charlotte precision.

Carolina Gandrud helps families navigate Charlotte's real estate market with confidence. As a realtor with Corcoran HM Properties, she brings a unique perspective shaped by international relocations and years managing complex operations for Fortune 500 companies.

Carolina is a highly accomplished and dynamic real estate agent who understands what families and individuals need when settling in a new city, with a proven track record of success. Her corporate background with companies like Tyco Electronics and The Hershey Company, combined with an MBA from Penn State, gives her sharp negotiation skills and the ability to navigate contract details clearly.

Her expertise, drive, and commitment make her the ideal partner for all your real estate needs, whether you're buying, selling, or investing.

Carolina analyzes pricing trends and with her extensive knowledge of the South Charlotte area and her strategic and organized approach, along with her systems and multitasking abilities, which set her apart from other brokers, she is the perfect broker to help you in your real estate goals.

Carolina is devoted to providing practical guidance and careful attention to her clients' success, resulting in multiple successful transactions. Fluent in English, Spanish, and some German, she connects easily with Charlotte's diverse population and welcomes relocating families from across the country and around the world.

Carolina knows what makes each Charlotte neighborhood unique. Working with Carolina guarantees a top-notch experience. She is dedicated to providing the highest level of customer service and ensuring your satisfaction throughout the buying or selling process. As a consistent top-producing agent, Carolina delivers amazing results with a streamlined process. You can expect exceptional service and remarkable outcomes when working with her.
