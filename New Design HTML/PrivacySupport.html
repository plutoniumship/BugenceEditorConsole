Common Questions
Where is my data stored?

Bugence data is encrypted and stored in secure AWS data centers located in the US-East (N. Virginia) region. We utilize multi-zone redundancy for high availability.

How do I export my project data?

You can use the "Export Personal Data" tool on this page. It will generate a downloadable JSON archive containing your project configurations, logs, and account details.

Who has access to my code?

Only you and your invited team members have access to your repositories. Bugence support staff do not access your source code unless explicitly authorized via a temporary support token.
